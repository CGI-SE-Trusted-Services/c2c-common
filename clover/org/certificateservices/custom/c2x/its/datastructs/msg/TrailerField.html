24    * may contain data of the following cases:
25    *
26    * <li> signature: the signature of this message contained in a Signature structure shall be given. The
27    * signature is calculated over the hash of the encoding of all previous fields (version, header_fields
28    * field and the payload_fields field), including the encoding of their length.
29    * If there is a payload field with type equal to signed_external, the data shall be included in the hash
30    * calculation immediately after the payload field, encoded as an opaque<var>, i.e. as if it was included.
31    * If there is one or more payload field whose type does not contain the keyword "signed" (unsecured or
32    * encrypted), the length fields shall be included, the corresponding data fields shall be excluded from the
33    * hash calculation.
34    *
35    * If further trailer fields are included in a SecuredMessage, the signature structure shall include all fields
36    * in the sequence before, and exclude all fields in the sequence after the signature structure, if not otherwise
