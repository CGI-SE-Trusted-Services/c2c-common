25    /**
26    * This structure defines how to give information about the signer of a message. The included cryptographic identity can
27    * be used in conjunction with the structure Signature to verify a message's authenticity. Depending on the value of
28    * type, the SignerInfo's data fields shall contain the following entries:
29    * <li> self: the data is self-signed. Therefore, no additional data shall be given. This shall only be used in case of a certificate request.
30    * <li> certificate_digest_with_ecdsap256: an 8 octet digest of the relevant certificate contained in a HashedId8 structure shall be given.
31    * <li> certificate: the relevant certificate itself contained in a Certificate structure shall be given.
32    * <li> certificate_chain: a complete certificate chain contained in a variable-length vector of type Certificate shall be given.
33    * The last element of the chain shall contain the certificate used to sign the message, the next to last element shall contain
34    * the certificate of the CA that signed the last certificate and so on. The first element of the chain needs not be a root certificate.
35    * <li>certificate_digest_with_other_algorithm: an 8 octet digest contained in a HashedId8 structure and the corresponding
36    * public key algorithm contained in a PublicKeyAlgorithm structure shall be given.
37    *
38    * @author Philip Vendil, p.vendil@cgi.com
39    *
40    */
